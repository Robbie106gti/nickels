FINISHED SIDES

General Information


  • Cabinets, by default, will have the outside of the gables (sides) unfinished, unless a Finished Side is specified.
  • Finished sides are ordered from the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu in Webquoin and will match the Exterior Material selected in the Exterior Mtrl Header Drop-Down Menu (see Screenshot 1).
  • Options include finishing the left or right of a cabinet (FE or IFE), adding a plant-on panel (PO), or adding an applied door (AD, ADP).
  • In WQ a finished side will automatically be sized and priced when a cabinet’s End Application is selected (left, right, left/right - See Screenshot 2) and will be shown in the Item List as an attachment to the cabinet

    WQ Screenshot

    Screenshot 1

    Click on the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu and choose ADP from the list. Next to the ADP selection, a second drop-down menu will appear with a door style that matches the Door Style Header Drop-Down Menu.

    Screenshot 2

    Editing a cabinet in the Item List will display the End Application Drop-Down Menu. Choose Left, Right or Left/Right from the list. Click Save to exit the Edit box.

    Screenshot 3

    The Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu selection will now display as an attachment on the cabinet you edited in the Item List.

To finish the top or bottom of a cabinet attach the Accessory code FCB (Finish Cabinet Bottom) or FCT (Finish Cabinet Top).

FE - Finished End

Category: Header Drop-Down Menu - Finish Side

Item Name: Finished End

Webquoin Code: FE


Application

  • Specified on a cabinet that is to have a specific side finished to match the Exterior Mtrl Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.
  • Grain (if applicable) will run vertically unless HG material is selected in the Exterior Mtrl Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.

Specifications

Available Materials: available in all Wood and Painted materials.

Finishing: finished left, right or left/right.

Size: automatically sized to depth and height of the cabinet side.

Pricing: square foot.

Construction: veneer for wood, MDF for painted applications.

Options: if required to be applied on-site order a VSKIN Accessory in WQ

Restrictions: not available in Knotty Woods (knotty material will be replaced with clear wood), all Melamines, or Painted Gloss (see WQ Tip).

To finish the side of a cabinet in Melamine or Painted Gloss use a PO.75P panel from the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu or manually attach a panel accessory, such as order code P.75P.

Ordering Information

  • Specify cabinet’s End Application, L (left), R (right) or L/R. Choose FE from the the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.

Screenshot 1

Click on the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu and choose FE from the list. Webquoin will automatically size the FE in the Item List.

IFE - Integrated Finished End

Category: Header Drop-Down Menu - Finish Side

Item Name: Integrated Finished End

Webquoin Code: IFE


Application

  • Specified on a cabinet that is to have a specific side finished with exactly the same material and finish on the interior (Interior Mtrl Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ) and the exterior (Exterior Mtrl Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ).
  • Grain (if applicable) will run vertically unless HG material is selected in the Exterior Mtrl Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.

Specifications

Available Materials: available in Solid, Patterned Melamine, Textured Melamine, M Maple and M White Melamine, Wood, Engineered and Painted materials.

Finishing: finished left, right or left/right.

Size: automatically sized to depth and height of the cabinet side.

Pricing: square foot.

Construction: veneer for wood, MDF for painted applications.

Options: none

Restrictions: not available in Knotty Woods (knotty material will be replaced with clear wood), Euro Textured Melamines (ETM), or Painted Gloss (see WQ Tip).

To finish the side of a cabinet in ETM (Euro Textured Melamine or Painted Gloss use a PO.75P panel from the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu or manually attach a panel accessory, such as order code P.75P.

Ordering Information

  • Specify cabinet’s End Application, L (left), R (right) or L/R. Choose IFE from the the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.

Screenshot 1

Click on the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu and choose IFE from the list. Webquoin will automatically size the IFE in the Item List.

PO.75P - Plant-On 3/4" Thick Panel

Category: Header Drop-Down Menu - Finish Side

Item Name: Plant-On 3/4" Thick Panel

Webquoin Code: PO.75P


Application

  • Plant-On panel is applied to the side of a cabinet.
  • Shipped loose and applied on-site.
  • Grain (if applicable) will run vertically unless HG material is selected in the Exterior Mtrl Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.

Specifications

Available Materials: available in all materials.

Finishing: finished on all faces and edges.

Size: automatically sized to depth of the cabinet side (+ 7/8”) and the height of the cabinet side. See WQ Tip

Pricing: square foot.

Construction: 3/4” thick composite core.

Options: none

Restrictions: not available in Knotty Woods (knotty material will be replaced with clear wood).

If a different size of panel is required, manually attach a panel accessory, such as order code P.75P.

Ordering Information

  • Specify cabinet’s End Application, L (left), R (right) or L/R. Choose PO.75P from the the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.

Screenshot 1

Click on the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu and choose PO.75P from the list. Webquoin will automatically size the PO in the Item List.

AD - Applied Door

Category: Header Drop-Down Menu - Finish Side

Item Name: Applied Door

Webquoin Code: AD


Application

  • AD is applied to the side of a cabinet.
  • Shipped loose and applied on-site.

Specifications

Available Materials: based on the specification of the door as selected in the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu

Finishing: finished on all faces and edges.

Size: automatically sized to depth of the cabinet side(- 1/8”) and the height of the cabinet side (- 1/8”). See WQ Tip.

Pricing: square foot.

Construction: based on the specification of the door as selected in the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu (starting on page 136). The side of a cabinet with an AD will be prepped with a matching edge tape.

Options: based on the specification of the door as selected in the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu

Restrictions: based on the specification of the door as selected in the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu

If a different size of door is required, manually add a door accessory, order code D (Door). Attach one of the following codes to the door: Customization Charge - Prep left side for AD, order code: CC-AL6, Customization Charge - Prep right side for AD, order code, CC-AR6 or Customization Charge - Prep back side for AD, order code: CC-AB6.

Ordering Information

  • Specify cabinet’s End Application, L (left) or R (right). Choose AD from the the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ.

Screenshot 1

Click on the Finish Side Header Drop-Down Menu and choose ADP from the list. Next to the ADP selection, a second drop-down menu will appear with a door style that matches the Door Style Header Drop-Down Menu.
