M White Dado Joint
(Modal line - M3D cabinet shown)


Description

Melamine White Dado Joint MOVENTO drawer system with an option for TIP-ON


Specifications

Size: A 24” Cabinet will have 21 5/8” standard depth whereas a 21” Cabinet will have a 19 5/8” of Standard full extension MOVENTO slide. See

Construction: A White Melamine drawer box with glue-reinforced blind dado joints made using 5/8” thick sides and bottoms.

Options:
  • Drawer slide depths available are:
    29 1/2", 27 5/8", 25 5/8", 23 5/8", 17 11/16", 15 3/4", 13 3/4", 11 1 3/16" and 10 5/8".
  • TIP-ON:
    For TIP-ON order code is DS-MWDJTOS for drawer faces up to 5 7/8” high, or DS-MWDJTOH for drawer faces over 5 7/8” high.

Restrictions: The drawer will be melamine white color.
