Specifications

Available Materials: All stained Wood and Painted materials.

Finishing:
  • STEP 1: Steps 1-8 of Stain Finishing Process or steps 1-6 of Paint Finishing Process.
  • STEP 2: The finished surface is scored with sandpaper in the direction of the grain.
  • STEP 3: A coating of glaze color is applied to the entire surface.
  • STEP 4: The surface is then wiped off and the glaze is left “hanging” in the profiled areas as well as the scored areas left by the sandpaper, which gives the surface the characteristic “brush” strokes.
  • STEP 5: The top-coat is applied to all glazed surfaces.
  • STEP 6: Final inspection for defects and color.

Size: not applicable

Pricing: percent up-charge on all finished products.

Construction: see Finishing

Options: Drag-Beach, Drag-Ebony, Drag-Espresso, Drag-Fawn, Drag-Pearl and Drag-Stone.

Restrictions: not available in Melamine or Laminate Gloss.

There is a random distribution of glaze residue left in the profiles of the surface. This is the most pronounced glaze option available.
A sample door with the drag glaze color is strongly recommended.

Ordering Information

  • On the Special Finish Header Drop-Down Menu in WQ, click on GLAZE and then DRAG GLAZE. Choose the Drag color from the list.
